Airport Visualizations

Map of Airports in Malaysia

This map shows the geographical distribution of airports in Malaysia. The size of each circle represents passenger traffic volume. This provides a broad overview of the locations and sizes of different airports in Malaysia.

Scatter Plot: Airport Traffic vs. Elevation

This scatter plot shows the correlation between the elevation of an airport and its traffic score.

Pie Chart: Airport Types in Malaysia

This pie chart visualizes the proportion of different airport types (e.g., large, medium, heliport) across Malaysia.

Bar Chart: Airports by Region

This bar chart displays the number of airports in each region of Malaysia, showing which regions have the most airports.
